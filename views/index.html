Grapes

Grapes are unique among fruits. Ripe, they contain sufficient sugar and an appropriate amount of acid so that when they ferment enough alcohol is produced to make a palatable wine that is protected against imminent spoilage. Other fruits do not possess the proper balance of these basic constituents.

Dr. SD Skikhamany & Dr B Srinivasulu
